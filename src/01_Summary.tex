\section*{Summary}\label{sec:summary}


%TOPIC: Todo empieza aqui
In the context of an undergoing transition towards circularity and sustainable waste management, the Netherlands, by means of the Ministry of Infrastructure and Water Management has deployed the Landelijk afvalbeheerplan (LAP3).
In this policy framework the national government has included incentives to further develop the investment in environmentally friendly assets.
This document was made to service on the petition for  Blue Cycle's prior investments to be included under Willekeurige afschrijving milieu-investeringen (Vamil) and Milieu-investeringsaftrek (MIA) subsidy schemes.
In line with that ambition, this undertaking may serve to justify and advocate for a deviation on incumbent waste hierarchical granting.
